VENDOR PORTAL

Dealing with several vendors for various purposes is difficult, thus we need to use a vendor management solution to assist us get more value out of our vendor relationships.

ERP Roots has created a robust and integrated solution for end-to-end procurement lifecycle with transparent collaboration between customer and vendor.

Vendor portal provides secure and flexible self-service access to vendors, reducing the productive time of employees spent on communicating the details of Purchase order, stock details, overdue shipments, open invoices, payment history, Goods/service delivery dates, quantity details, quality compliance, and RTP materials. Allow your personnel to focus on more strategic efforts instead of dealing with routine vendor enquiries.
